About Me

Driven by Systems Thinking

I'm a Software Engineer II (L62) at Microsoft, currently working on the Accelerated DPU DirectDrive (Ultra-Disk) DataPath team in Bengaluru, India.


My work focuses on building NVMe-based virtual disk architecture for Azure Cloud, RDMA-enabled high-performance data paths, and storage slicing, replication & coordination on FunOS-powered DPUs.


With 6.7+ years across top tech companies, I've built ASR9K router features at Cisco, developed automotive ECU systems for BMW, Audi & Porsche at Bosch, and contributed to OneFS distributed storage at Dell.
